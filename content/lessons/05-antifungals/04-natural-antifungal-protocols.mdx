## Why Choose Natural Antifungals?

Natural antifungals can be highly effective for fungal colonization, though they typically require longer treatment periods than prescription options. They're often better tolerated with fewer side effects.

**Best candidates for natural approach:**
- Mild to moderate colonization
- Can't access prescriptions
- Liver concerns
- Prefer natural medicine
- Have 3-6 months for treatment
- Failed or intolerant to prescriptions

## Rotation Strategies

### Why Rotate?

**Prevents resistance:**
- Fungi adapt to single agents
- Rotation prevents adaptation
- Maintains effectiveness
- Better long-term results

**Reduces side effects:**
- Gives body breaks
- Prevents tolerance
- Minimizes die-off
- More sustainable

**Die-off management:**

- Molybdenum 250mcg daily
- Vitamin C to bowel tolerance

**Gut restoration:**
- Probiotics (2 hours from antifungals)
- L-glutamine 5g daily
- Digestive enzymes with meals
- Slippery elm or aloe

## Duration and Expectations

### Timeline for Natural Protocols

**Month 1:**
- Initial die-off (Days 3-10)
- Adjustment period
- Mild improvements
- Finding right doses

**Month 2:**
- Die-off subsiding
- Energy improving
- Symptoms decreasing
- Better digestion

**Month 3:**
- Significant improvement
- Clearer thinking
- Less fungal symptoms
- Consider adding/rotating

**Months 4-6:**
- Continued improvement
- May need to continue
- Assess for completion
- Plan maintenance

## When Natural Isn't Enough

### Signs to switch to prescription:

- No improvement after 2 months
- Severe symptoms
- Systemic infection signs
- Can't tolerate die-off
- Need faster results

### Combining with prescription:

**Lower dose prescription + natural:**
- Reduces side effects
- Better tolerance
- Broader coverage
- More sustainable

**Example combination:**
- Itraconazole 100mg daily (half dose)
- Neem 500mg 2x daily
- Caprylic acid 1000mg 2x daily

## Maintenance After Treatment

### Preventing Recurrence

**Ongoing support:**
- Probiotics daily
- Rotate natural antifungals monthly
- Low sugar/mold diet
- Environmental vigilance

**Pulse protocols:**
- One week per month antifungals
- Or 3 days weekly
- Prevents recolonization
- Maintains gains
