## Your 4-Week Drainage Opening Protocol

### Week 1-2: Foundation
- Focus on hydration and bowel movements
- Begin gentle movement (walking, stretching)
- Start liver support supplements
- Introduce warm lemon water morning routine

### Week 2-3: Amplification
- Add dry brushing or rebounding
- Increase movement intensity
- Begin sauna or hot baths (2-3x/week)
- Introduce castor oil packs

### Week 3-4: Optimization
- All pathways should be functioning
- Sauna 4-5x per week if tolerated
- Consistent daily bowel movements
- Check drainage readiness score daily

## Signs Your Drainage Pathways Are Open

### ✅ Ready to Progress:
- Daily bowel movements (1-3x)
- Urinating regularly (pale yellow)
- Sweating during exercise/sauna
- No swelling or puffiness
- Energy improving (not declining)
- Drainage score ≥80% for 7 consecutive days

### ❌ Not Ready Yet:
- Constipation or irregular bowels
- Dark urine or infrequent urination
- Can't break a sweat
- Feeling increasingly fatigued
- New symptoms appearing
- Lymph nodes swollen
